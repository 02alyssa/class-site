screenprinting in *silence*

This is an iteration of the first discussion on screenprinting in silence. This iteration focuses on exhausting a similar practice of silence, concentration, and the pure pleasure of the printmaking process.

  • here are some practices for printing in silence:
  • find and open space
  • choose a space that is silent
  • optional: listen to some calming music while printing (preferably with headphones)
  • quietly enjoy the process

Thank you for reading and taking the time to visit this site. My goal is that by practicing many other forms of being in silence, like eating, reading, sleeping, we can learn to appreciate quiet moments of making. Please continue to enjoy small pockets of silence in your life.
