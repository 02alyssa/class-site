*screenprinting in silence*

What I aim to do in my printing practice: include playful colors, experiment with layouts on a variety of mediums, and choosing new colors of ink.

This is why silent screenprinting is my love and passion: it serves as a therapeutic quietness. You're creating something entirtely new in real life, as well as finishing a project with something beautiful.

  • materials needed to screenprint in silence:
  • ink
  • paper
  • screen
  • acetate
  • painter's tape
  • a silent workplace
